For Gmail Smart Compose, deterministic generation methods are chosen over stochastic ones because they offer:

Consistency: Users expect predictable suggestions that don't change drastically for the same input,
 ensuring a reliable experience.
Focus on common phrases: In an email context, the goal is often to suggest the most probable and conventional 
completions, which deterministic methods prioritize.
Reduced risk of inappropriate suggestions: By avoiding randomness, deterministic methods minimize the chance 
of generating unexpected or unsuitable text, which is critical for professional communication like emails.
Within deterministic methods, the two main algorithms to consider are 
Greedy search and Beam search.
